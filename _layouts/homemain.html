The Rails for the Internet of Markets

Founded by Bram Cohen, inventor of BitTorrent, Chia is a next-generation open source blockchain built from the ground up to meet the needs of the quickly evolving internet of connected markets. Building and expanding on the best parts of existing solutions, Chia takes blockchain and cryptocurrency to a new level by focusing on compliance, sustainability and programmability, while greatly enhancing security.

Sustainable and Secure Circular Drive Initiative

As a founding member of the Circular Drive Initiative, we join William McDonough and the industry in declaring a commitment to reduce e-waste by supporting the secure reuse of storage hardware. Chia netspace represents over 40 exabytes with over 350,000 nodes. We support the opportunity to securely reduce e-waste by giving drives a second use in Chia farming.

Authentically secure, sustainable and compliant.

Secure

Chia is the most decentralized blockchain with about 350,000 nodes. It has the highest replication with all transactions and data being securely stored and tracked on every node. The ChiaLisp programming language meets all the features of other languages while being far more auditable, and scalable.

Compliant

Chia believes that in order to deliver on the promise of the next generation blockchains, they must adhere to regulatory compliance rules. It is our stated goal to be a public reporting company. We intend to be in compliance with Securities Act of 33, Sarbanes Oxley and other relevant existing practices.

Sustainable

With its innovative Proof Of Space and Time Chia uses less than 1% of the energy usage of Bitcoin. We are committed to the circular economy and are a part of the Circular Drive Initiative that encourages extension of drive lifespan and recycling.
